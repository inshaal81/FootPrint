About Us
We built a privacy dashboard that helps people take control of their digital footprint.
Our digital lives leave behind a trail of personal data—emails, phone numbers, trackers, and browsing activity—often without us noticing. That’s why we created Footprint, a Digital Privacy Dashboard that turns confusing privacy info into clear, actionable insights. With Footprint, you can check whether your email or phone number has appeared in a data breach, see the cookies and trackers following you across websites, and visualize your “digital shadow”—the companies that may be holding your data. We also generate a simple privacy risk score to help you understand your exposure at a glance.
Always happy to hear feedback or suggestions at footprint.teammm@gmail.com.
